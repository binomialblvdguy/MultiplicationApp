# VEDIC RATIOS: 2x3 Mastery

When multiplying a 2-digit number by a 3-digit number, we look for a **Split Point** that creates a simple ratio.

## 1. THE POWER OF 1
If you see a **1** anywhere, that is your starting point!

### If the 1 is in the 2-digit number:
Designate this as your multiplier. You will have 2 lines of calculation.
* **Example: 12 x 345**
* Prefix is **345** (1 x 345)
* Suffix is **690** (2 x 345)

**The Grade School Stack:**
690  (Suffix)
345    (Prefix)
-------
4140

### If the 1 is in the 3-digit number:
Look for the 1 in the middle! It creates 3 parts.
* **Example: 97 x 213**
* Middle is 97 (1 x 97)
* Left side (c) is 2 x 97 = 194
* Right side (a) is 3 x 97 = 291

**The Grade School Stack:**
291
97
194
---------
20661
