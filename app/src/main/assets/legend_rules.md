This app multiplies two 2-digit numbers (10–99).

AUTO:
The app looks for an easy shortcut rule first.
Examples:
• 12 and 21 are treated as the same ratio family.
• 25 × 41 can be read as 25 × (40 + 1).
• 25 × 91 can be read as 25 × (90 + 1).

STANDARD:
This uses the Vertical & Crosswise method directly.

How to use:
1. Enter two 2-digit numbers.
2. Choose AUTO or STANDARD.
3. Tap CALCULATE.
4. Read the answer and the worked steps below.

Notes:
• AUTO may choose a shortcut rule when one number has an easy pattern.
• STANDARD always shows Vertical & Crosswise.
• The app is limited to 2-digit multiplication only.
